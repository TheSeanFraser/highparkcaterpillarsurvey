## Join the High Park Caterpillar Survey

**Beginners are welcome!**

Reasons to join:
- See numerous species up close
- Learn about caterpillar ecology & natural history
- Observe other organisms that fluoresce
- Meet like-minded nature enthusiasts
- Contribute to citizen science
- Regular participants receive a [High Park caterpillar poster](/poster/)

[Read the FAQs](/faq/) for more information.

![UV caterpillars](/assets/images/uv-caterpillars.jpg)

[Check out this post](/Night-Session-Routine/) for a sense of how a night session typically goes.

## Session Start Times
As the season proceeds from mid-May through mid-October (start and end dates dependant on weather), the initial session start time is 9:15 PM - gradually becoming earlier starting in mid-summer as the sun starts to set earlier.



## You need to be
- 18 years of age or older
- available to do evening sessions (see session times below)
- able to walk up to 4 km per session on paved & unpaved surfaces (most sessions are 1-3 km)
